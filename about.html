About Us at GoodReads

Ever wanted to just pick up a good book, randomly pick up a magazine or comic and relax with the knowledge that your going to enjoy it, to be able to find a similar selection of items that is just as good as the first and at your finger tips. This is the mission here at GoodReads to go through the insurmountable levels of reading material afforded to us in the digital age and locate the most interesting, well thought through collections around.

Starting life as a small project the demand for entertainment has never been higher and whatever the type of material as long as you can read it we are dedicated to making it available for anyone who wants it.

Childhood books that helped you sleep at night to the collective works of the world war.

Weekly cooking magazines to help you with your favourite recipes or maybe ways to improve your creative skills.

Your favourite comics to delight your inner child or gruesome manga we have it right here.

Join us and our community in the continuous search for good entertainment. Have anything you remember and wish to see available again? How about something you wish you had the opportunity to read? Or maybe you just want to chat about your favourite works? See below for our social media links or our contact page if you want to speak directly.
