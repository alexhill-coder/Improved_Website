How to Contact Us

To contact us please message us by filling out the form below:

  • Please provide your email address
  • Select the appropriate header for your message so the appropriate team can help you.
  • General - If you want to discuss any of the collections.
  • Enquiry - If you have any works that you want us to digitize.
  • Issues - If you have discovered an issue with any of the works on the site.
  • Other - For anything that doesn't fall under the other categories.
  • Write a your message in the text field and be as detailed as you can so we can better assess the situation.
  • Please consider subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Finally submit your message and we'll get in touch as soon as we can.
We'll never share your email with anyone else.
